ROBBERIES BY
TIME RANGE

The time of day is one of the variables that can have the greatest impact on the likelihood of theft for a shipment. This month, we have detected an increase in thefts between 20:01 and 2:00 hrs (last month this time period accounted for 25% of thefts), indicating that as the night progresses, different shipments may be in vulnerable situations.
