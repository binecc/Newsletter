Comparing figures at the national level between the SESNSP and AI27, we can observe similarities between the regions of the country with the highest number of robberies, being the Central and the Bajío areas with the highest number of robberies.​ ​

According to Secretariat figures, 86% of thefts committed to transporters are committed with violence; being the State of Mexico and Puebla the most violent states with 39% and 28% of violent thefts.​ ​

We note that, at the end of the third quarter of 2023, SESNSP does not report theft events in states such as Guerrero, Nayarit and Queretaro, reinforcing the importance of reporting this crime in the relevant instances.
